Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

This is one of the simplest and most classic Italian pasta dishes, relying on just a few ingredients: garlic, olive oil, chili flakes, and parsley. Itâ€™s quick, inexpensive, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp red chili flakes
  • Salt, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta

    Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, then drain.

  2. Prepare the sauce

    In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic slices and cook gently until golden (not burnt). Add chili flakes and stir.

  3. Combine

    Add drained spaghetti and a splash of pasta water to the pan. Toss well to coat in the garlicky oil.

  4. Serve

    Top with chopped parsley and serve immediately.
