Ravioli

Description

In this recipe, we’ll use store-bought cheese ravioli and a quick tomato sauce made with pantry staples. The result is a dish that’s rich, flavorful, and perfect for a weeknight dinner. Even if you’re new to cooking, you’ll find this recipe approachable and rewarding

Ingredients

  • 1 package (250–300g) fresh cheese ravioli (store-bought)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil (or a few fresh leaves)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the ravioli

    Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add the ravioli and cook according to the package instructions (usually 3–4 minutes). Drain and set aside.

  2. Make the sauce

    Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Combine Add the cooked ravioli to the sauce. Gently toss to coat without breaking them.

  4. Serve

    Plate the ravioli, sprinkle with Parmesan, and serve hot.
